Traveling with all senses

Imagine you are sitting in a train compartment on a long journey. What do you percieve?

Does everyone perveive travelling by train in the same way? We want to investigate this question and allow for comparison of perceptions using colours. They make our feelings visible. To get the broadest insight possibel, we've sent this survey to sedign students from all over Europe.

Instructions:

  1. Do not overthink, just follow your intuition
  2. Please help us in making this project even better by sharing this project
  3. This should only take up to 5 minutes of your time!

Thank you very much for participating! So, let's go!
